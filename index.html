Frogger by mkaydub

How to play:

Use the arrow keys on your keyboard in order to move your player from block to block. Your objective is to make it to the water and swim! If you make it to the water 5 times without being hit by an enemy, you win the game! If you are hit by an enemy your star count will return to zero, but your timer will not!

Hit escape (esc) on your keyboard to exit this screen and start the game!
