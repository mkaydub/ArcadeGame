Here's how to play:

This is mkaydub's version of the classic arcade game Frogger.

The Rules: Use your arrow keys in order to move your player. Your objective is to make it to the water and swim! If you make it to the water 5 times without being hit by an enemy, you win the game! If you are hit by an enemy your star count will return to zero, but your timer will not!
